Instructions

Transcribe the English Speech

1. Ignore ALL punctuation marks such as period, comma, quotation, question, etc. BUT KEEP apostrophe. For example "what's", "I'm", "it's", "I've", "you're", "I've".

2. Ignore ALL non-textual phrases such as "aha", "ha", "um", "umms", "hmms", "uhs", etc.

3. Put "?" at the word that is not clear to you. Too many "?" or errors will be rejected.

4. Transcribe numbers using word: e.g. 2 -> two, 34 -> thirty four, 576 -> five hundred senventy six.
